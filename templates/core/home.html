Who are we?

We are a teen-based nonprofit that prepares and partners with local restaurants to provide food, then we pack it with care and deliver it to people in need, including those experiencing homelessness. We collect leftovers of unused food from restaurants that would otherwise go to waste, turning it into meals for people who need it most.

What we do

We reach out to local businesses willing to donate food or supplies — fresh, properly stored, or unopened food ONLY — organize volunteers to pack everything into meal boxes, and distribute them to homeless individuals and families in need.

Why we do it

Millions of pounds of food go to waste every year while people in our communities go hungry. StirForChange bridges that gap, turning restaurant food waste into meals for people.

Why it matters

Hunger isn't a distant problem — it's happening on streets near you. When a restaurant donates its leftover food to us instead of throwing it away, that food becomes someone's dinner tonight. Every box we deliver is a direct act of change in our community.

Why you should care

Because you can be part of the solution. If you're a restaurant, your excess food has value — we'll make sure it reaches someone who needs it. If you're a volunteer, one afternoon of packing and delivering can feed dozens of people.

The impact

StirForChange tackles two problems at once: community hunger and food waste. Every meal we deliver reduces the amount of food that ends up in a landfill and puts it where it belongs — on the table of someone in need.
